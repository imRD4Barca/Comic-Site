Hunter x Hunter is a legendary shonen manga by Yoshihiro Togashi.
It follows Gon Freecss as he embarks on a quest to find his father and become a Hunter.
With mind-bending battles, deep friendships, and a world full of danger and wonder, Hunter x Hunter is a story about growth, ambition, and the true meaning of strength.

Iconic Characters

Gon Freecss

An energetic and kind-hearted boy whose relentless optimism drives him to become a Hunter and find his father.

Killua Zoldyck

A prodigy assassin with lightning-fast reflexes, Killua is Gon’s best friend and loyal companion.

Kurapika

The last of the Kurta Clan, Kurapika seeks vengeance against the Phantom Troupe for his people’s massacre.

Leorio Paradinight

Aspiring doctor and comic relief, Leorio’s loyalty and courage make him an irreplaceable friend.

Alluka Zoldyck

Killua’s mysterious sibling, Alluka’s wish-granting powers are both a blessing and a curse.

Biscuit Krueger

Don’t let her looks fool you—Bisky is a master Nen user and a tough mentor to Gon and Killua.

Ging Freecss

Gon’s elusive father and a legendary Hunter, Ging is a master of adventure and mystery.

Kite

A skilled Hunter and Gon’s mentor, Kite’s calm wisdom and unpredictable weapon make him a force to be reckoned with.

Morel Mackernasey

A veteran Hunter and master strategist, Morel’s smoke-based Nen abilities and calm under pressure make him a formidable ally in any battle.

Feitan Portor

The Phantom Troupe’s sadistic interrogator, Feitan is swift, ruthless, and shrouded in mystery.

Nobunaga Hazama

The Phantom Troupe’s swordsman, Nobunaga’s lightning-fast draw and unwavering loyalty make him a deadly force among the Spiders.

Uvogin

A powerhouse of the Phantom Troupe, Uvogin’s brute strength and love of battle make him nearly unstoppable in close combat.

Illumi Zoldyck

A master manipulator and assassin, Illumi’s cold logic and needle techniques make him one of the most dangerous Zoldycks.

Hisoka

A deadly magician obsessed with strong opponents, Hisoka’s motives are as mysterious as his powers.

Chrollo Lucilfer

The enigmatic leader of the Phantom Troupe, Chrollo is a genius strategist and formidable foe.

Neferpitou

A Royal Guard of the Chimera Ant King, Pitou’s catlike curiosity hides terrifying strength and loyalty.

Shaiapouf

The butterfly Royal Guard, Pouf is fiercely devoted to the King and will do anything to protect him—even at the cost of his own sanity.

Menthuthuyoupi

The powerhouse Royal Guard, Youpi’s monstrous rage and adaptability make him a nearly unstoppable force on the battlefield.

Zeno Zoldyck

Killua’s grandfather and a legendary assassin, Zeno’s experience and mastery of Nen make him a force to be feared.

Isaac Netero

The legendary Chairman of the Hunter Association, Netero’s strength, speed, and spirit are unmatched even in his old age.

Meruem

The Chimera Ant King, Meruem is a symbol of overwhelming power and evolving humanity.

Yoshihiro Togashi

The creator of Hunter x Hunter, known for his brilliant storytelling, complex characters, and unpredictable plot twists.
Togashi’s work has inspired countless fans and set new standards for shonen manga.

Yoshihiro Togashi is the author and artist of Hunter x Hunter, which began serialization in 1998 and has become a beloved classic.
The manga inspired a hit anime adaptation, movies, and a passionate global fanbase.
Togashi is celebrated for his creativity, depth, and the emotional impact of his stories.

In the words of Togashi:

"The world is not perfect, but it’s there for us trying the best it can. That’s what makes it so damn beautiful."
